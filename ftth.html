Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between FTTH and FTTB?

FTTH (Fiber to the Home) is designed for residential properties, while FTTB (Fiber to the Business) is optimized for commercial properties with features like higher upload speeds, static IPs, and business-grade SLAs.

How long does installation take?

Standard installation typically takes 2-4 hours, depending on the complexity of the setup. We aim to complete all installations within 5-7 business days from order confirmation.

Is there a contract period?

We offer both month-to-month and 12/24-month contract options. Longer contracts come with additional benefits such as discounted rates or free equipment.

What happens if fiber is not available in my area?

If fiber is not yet available in your area, we offer alternative solutions such as wireless connectivity. You can also register your interest, and we'll notify you when fiber becomes available in your location.

Do you provide the router?

Yes, our premium packages include a free Wi-Fi router. For other packages, you can either purchase a router from us or use your own compatible device.
