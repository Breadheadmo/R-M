Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order quantity for branded merchandise?

Minimum order quantities vary by product type. Most items have a minimum order of 50-100 units, but some premium products may have lower minimums. Our starter package begins at R5,000 and includes a selection of products to meet your needs.

How long does the production process take?

Standard production time is typically 7-14 business days after design approval, depending on the products and quantities ordered. Rush production is available for most items at an additional cost, with turnaround times as quick as 3-5 business days for urgent needs.

Do you provide design services if we don't have artwork ready?

Yes, our in-house design team can create custom artwork for your branded merchandise. We offer various levels of design services, from basic logo placement to complete creative concepts. We work closely with you to ensure the designs align with your brand guidelines and marketing objectives.

Can I see samples before placing a large order?

Absolutely. We offer pre-production samples for most products so you can evaluate the quality and appearance before committing to a full order. Sample costs vary by product and are typically credited toward your final order when you proceed with production.

Do you offer warehousing and fulfillment services?

Yes, we provide comprehensive fulfillment services including warehousing, inventory management, and distribution. This is particularly valuable for businesses with ongoing merchandise needs, multiple locations, or regular events. Our Business and Corporate packages include various levels of fulfillment support.
