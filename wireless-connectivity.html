Frequently Asked Questions

How many access points will I need for my business?

The number of access points depends on several factors including the size of your space, building materials, expected user density, and required throughput. Our site survey process will determine the optimal number and placement of access points for your specific environment.

How secure are your wireless solutions?

Our wireless solutions implement enterprise-grade security including WPA3 encryption, 802.1X authentication, rogue AP detection, intrusion prevention, and secure guest access. We also offer regular security audits and updates to protect against emerging threats.

Can you integrate with our existing network infrastructure?

Yes, our wireless solutions are designed to integrate seamlessly with your existing network infrastructure. We work with all major vendors and can implement solutions that complement your current setup while providing a path for future upgrades.

Do you offer ongoing support and maintenance?

Yes, we offer comprehensive support and maintenance packages that include 24/7 monitoring, regular firmware updates, performance optimization, and technical support. Our proactive approach helps identify and resolve potential issues before they impact your business.

How long does implementation typically take?

Implementation timelines vary based on the size and complexity of the project. A small office setup can be completed in 1-2 days, while larger enterprise deployments may take 1-2 weeks. Our project managers will provide a detailed timeline during the planning phase.
