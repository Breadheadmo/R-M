About R&M Group

Your trusted partner in telecommunications and business solutions since 2011

WHO WE ARE

Leading the Way in Telecommunications

R&M Group (Pty) Ltd is a dynamic, privately-owned telecommunications company headquartered in Midrand, Johannesburg, Gauteng, South Africa. Founded in 2011, the company has steadily grown to become a trusted partner in the ICT and fibre broadband sector.

We specialize in delivering bespoke internet and connectivity solutions, fibre reseller services, and professional fibre installation for a wide range of clients—from agile start-ups to established large-scale enterprises. Our approach combines technical expertise with a hands-on, consultative service model that prioritizes the client experience at every stage.

WHAT WE OFFER

Our Comprehensive Services

Sales & Internet Solutions

We work closely with businesses to assess their internet requirements and provide tailored connectivity packages that offer both flexibility and performance. From small teams working remotely to large office networks requiring dedicated high-speed links, our internet solutions are built for productivity.

Fibre Reseller Services

R&M Group acts as a reliable fibre reseller, leveraging strategic partnerships with major network providers to offer competitive fibre packages. We manage the procurement, provisioning, and ongoing service delivery, ensuring seamless integration into our clients' operations.

Fibre Installations

Our team of certified technicians and engineers handle full-scope fibre installation projects—from initial consultation and site surveys to trenching, cabling, and activation. Every installation is carried out with precision, safety, and minimal disruption to client operations.
