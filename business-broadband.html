Frequently Asked Questions

How is business broadband different from regular broadband?

Business broadband offers dedicated bandwidth, symmetrical speeds, static IP addresses, enhanced security features, priority support, and service level agreements that guarantee uptime and performance—all essential features for business operations that aren't typically available with consumer broadband packages.

What does the SLA cover?

Our Service Level Agreements cover guaranteed uptime percentages, maximum response times for support issues, resolution timeframes, and performance metrics like latency and packet loss. Compensation is provided if we fail to meet these agreed standards.

Can I upgrade my package as my business grows?

Yes, we offer flexible upgrade paths that allow you to scale your connectivity as your business grows. In most cases, upgrades can be implemented without changing your physical infrastructure, minimizing disruption to your operations.

Do you provide equipment for business connections?

Yes, all our business packages include enterprise-grade routers and necessary equipment. For larger implementations, we can also provide managed switches and Wi-Fi access points as part of a comprehensive networking solution.

What security features are included?

Our security features include DDoS protection, advanced firewall services, intrusion detection and prevention, secure VPN options, content filtering, and regular security audits. Higher-tier packages include more comprehensive security suites.
